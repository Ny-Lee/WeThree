Return Policy


If you're not 100% satisfied with your WeThree purchase, you can return it for any reason.

Your purchase must be returned in saleable condition with the original packaging, including Universal Product Code (UPC), manuals parts and your receipt or packing slip. Any personal information should be removed from the ite before returning to the store.

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 30 days in saleable condition in its original packaging (including UPC code, manual, parts and your receipt or packing slip) for full refund or replacement. The tender will be mabde to the same tender as the purchase. Debit and credit card refunds will only be processed to the dame card number used for the original purchase. Items purchased by cheque and returned within 14 days or purchase may be returned for a store credit only. Personal information is to be removed from any item before returning it to the store.

If you do not have a receipt, you may exchange the product or you will be issued a store credit provided that the purchase can be verified. The store credit will be the lowest retail price of the item over the last 90 days. In the absence of proof of purchase, identification may be required. WeThree reserves the right to decline a return should proof of purchase not be verifiable.
